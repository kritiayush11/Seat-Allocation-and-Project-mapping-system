### Employees (5,001)
- **Employee code:** `ETH-00001` through `ETH-05001`

- **Department:** Engineering, Security, HR, Finance, Operations, etc.
- **Role:** ML Engineer, Data Analyst, Project Manager, etc.
- **Status:** Mostly `ACTIVE`, some `INACTIVE`, `ON_LEAVE`
- **project_id:** Each employee assigned to one of the 11 projects
